About the role

You'll own outbound and inbound lead flow for a 3–8 attorney personal injury firm. This is a hands-on role: write the creative, run the campaigns, qualify every inbound, and book the walkthrough on the partner's calendar. No agency layer between you and the result.

What you'll do

  • Run PI-specific paid campaigns (auto, premises, work-injury)
  • Answer every inbound under 5 min — text, call, email
  • Qualify against firm-approved criteria; surface only signal
  • Book 25–35 qualified walkthroughs per month
  • Log every disposition; weekly SLA dashboard to partners
  • Replace existing agency, SDR, and CRM tooling spend

Requirements

  • 5+ years operating a PI firm or its marketing budget
  • Currently spending $8K–$15K/mo on generic marketing
  • Tolerance for ghost-lead callbacks < 5%
  • You've fired at least one agency in the last 24 months
  • 3+ attorneys on staff
  • Willingness to track CPL down to the case type

Why this role exists

Most firms hire generic marketing agencies. Most generic agencies don't book walkthroughs. This role exists to fix that.
