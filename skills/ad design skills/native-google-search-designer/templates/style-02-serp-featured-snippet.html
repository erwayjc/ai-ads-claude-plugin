How can personal injury firms get leads to call back the same day?
  • Wire the intake line directly to a 5-minute-or-less SLA monitored by the partner, not by an answering service
  • Replace email-only confirmations with a 3-touch system: text, call, calendar invite
  • Audit the intake script for fairness language — vague scripts cost roughly 30–40% of qualified leads
  • Most PI firms can re-engineer this in a single 30-minute teardown
