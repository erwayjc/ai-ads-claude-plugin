You are creating a new ad campaign in this workspace.

Invoke the **campaign-manager** skill. Ask the user the questions needed to fill in `CAMPAIGN.md` (project, ICP, offer, supporting points, words to use / avoid, creative direction). Don't create the folder until you have answers — a half-filled campaign brief is worse than no brief.

Once the brief is complete, add a row to `campaigns/CAMPAIGNS.md`.

After scaffolding, tell the user what they can do next: "build a bold-text ad", "build a Gemini ad", "build a chat-style ad", or "build a system-visual ad".
